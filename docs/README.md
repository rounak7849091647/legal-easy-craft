## Project Summary

**LegalCareAI** is an AI-powered legal intelligence platform designed specifically for Indian law. The platform provides:

- **AI Legal Assistant (CARE)** - Conversational AI with voice support for legal queries
- **Multi-language Support** - 11 Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, etc.
- **Lawyer Directory** - 10M+ Bar Council verified lawyers across all Indian states
- **Document Templates** - 100+ ready-to-use legal document templates
- **Tax Services** - ITR filing, tax planning, and CA-assisted services
- **Community Forum** - Legal discussions and community support
- **Dashboard** - Case management and calendar for registered users

## Unique Innovations

1. **Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS) 2023 Integration** - First AI system with complete BNS knowledge
